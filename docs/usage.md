# User Guide

## Dashboard

The dashboard gives you a quick overview of your job search status. You can see:
- Total active pipelines
- Upcoming interviews
- Recent activity

## Managing Pipelines

A **Pipeline** represents a specific job application. 

### Creating a Pipeline
1. Navigate to the **Pipelines** tab.
2. Click **Add Pipeline**.
3. Enter the Company Name, Role, and current Status.

### Tracking Progress
Click on any pipeline to view details, add notes, or change its status (e.g., from "Applied" to "Interviewing").

## Interview Management

Schedule and track your interviews.

### Scheduling
1. Go to **Interviews** or open a specific Pipeline.
2. Click **Schedule Interview**.
3. Select the date, time, and type of interview (Technical, HR, etc.).

### Google Calendar Sync
If you have set up Google Integration, your interviews can be automatically added to your Google Calendar. This can be toggled in **Settings**.

## Question Bank

Prepare for interviews by tracking common questions.

1. Go to **Questions**.
2. Add new questions you've encountered or want to practice.
3. You can categorize them (e.g., Behavioral, Technical) and add your answers.

## Settings & Sync

Configure the application in the **Settings** tab.

- **Sync Status**: Shows if you are online and if your data is being synced.
- **Sync Mode**:
    - *Local Only*: Data stays on your machine.
    - *Sync on Change*: Updates are sent to Google Sheets immediately.
    - *Sync Periodically*: Updates are batched.
- **Data Management**: View where your local database is stored.
