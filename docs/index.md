# Welcome to Interview Tracker

Interview Tracker is a modern, cross-platform desktop application designed to help you organize and track your job search process.

## Key Features

- **Pipeline Management**: Track applications across different stages (Applied, Interviewing, Offer, etc.).
- **Interview Scheduling**: Manage upcoming interviews with integrated calendar views.
- **Question Bank**: Store and organize interview questions and answers.
- **Offline First**: Works completely offline. Your data is safe on your device.
- **Google Integration**: (Optional) Sync your data with Google Sheets and Google Calendar.
